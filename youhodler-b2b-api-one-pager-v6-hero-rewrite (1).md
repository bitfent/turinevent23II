# FAQ

### What is a digital asset infrastructure provider? [expandable]

A digital asset infrastructure provider offers the core systems for managing digital assets — including accounts, payments, custody, conversion, and settlement — through APIs and platforms used by regulated businesses.

---

### How do I integrate stablecoin payments into my product? [expandable]

You can integrate stablecoin payments through an API platform that provides client accounts, on-ramp and off-ramp, payouts, and settlement, without building the full infrastructure or licensing stack yourself.

---

### Do I need a license to offer stablecoin or digital asset services in the EU? [expandable]

Under MiCA, offering crypto-asset services in the EU requires proper authorization. Many businesses use a licensed infrastructure provider to launch or continue services without becoming a regulated entity themselves.

### What is YouHodler Business API? [expandable]

YouHodler Business API is digital asset infrastructure for businesses that move money for customers. It enables client accounts, stablecoin payments, payouts, on-ramp, off-ramp, conversion, settlement, governance, and reporting inside your product.

---

### Who is it built for? [expandable]

Payment institutions, EMIs, cross-border businesses, fintechs, embedded finance platforms, treasury teams, and teams building automation or agentic finance flows.

---

### What flows can I launch? [expandable]

Client accounts, funding, stablecoin payments, payouts, on-ramp, off-ramp, conversion, and settlement — all inside your product.

---

### How does this help with MiCA? [expandable]

MiCA changes how stablecoin and digital asset services operate in the EU. YouHodler provides a licensed infrastructure model to launch or continue these services without rebuilding the full stack.

---

### How fast can we start? [expandable]
