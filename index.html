Why now

Digital assets are becoming a board-level business topic

The challenges have not changed: moving money across borders, managing liquidity, operating across currencies and reducing friction in financial operations. What is changing are the tools companies can use to solve them.

This event is designed for business leaders who want a practical, non-technical view of where digital assets can create value today — and where they cannot.

  • 01

    Move faster across markets

    Understand how digital asset rails can support international operations and faster settlement models.

  • 02

    Manage liquidity with flexibility

    Explore how companies can think about fiat, stablecoins and digital assets as part of a modern treasury toolkit.

  • 03

    Reduce operational friction

    See how new financial infrastructure can simplify payment flows, conversion and reconciliation.

Practical use cases

Built around real corporate needs

This is not a technical workshop and not a crypto trading event. It is a business conversation about practical financial use cases.

  • Cross-border payments

    How companies can move value internationally with fewer delays and clearer settlement expectations.

  • Multi-currency operations

    How businesses can think about EUR, USD, GBP, CHF and digital assets inside a single operational strategy.

  • On-ramp & off-ramp

    How fiat and digital assets can move in both directions when companies need speed and control.

  • Corporate accounts

    How business accounts can support compliant access to digital asset operations.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is the event open to the public?

No. This is an invitation-only business event for selected corporate guests and decision makers.

Who should attend?

CEOs, CFOs, founders, entrepreneurs and senior decision makers who manage international operations, payments, liquidity or corporate finance strategy.

Do I need to understand crypto already?

No. The event is designed for business leaders who want a practical introduction to digital assets in a corporate context.

Is the derby ticket included?

Yes. Confirmed participants will receive a complimentary ticket for Torino vs Juventus. VIP skybox access is limited and subject to availability.

When will my application be confirmed?

The YouHodler team will review applications manually and contact selected guests by email.

Will YouHodler present a new business product?

Selected guests may receive a private preview of what YouHodler is building for businesses. Full details will be shared only when approved.
