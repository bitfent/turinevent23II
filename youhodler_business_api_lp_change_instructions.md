# YouHodler Business API Landing Page - Change Instructions

Target landing page: https://business-api.youhodler.com/
Source instruction document: `30.04.2026- API landing page change of vocab - Sheet1.pdf`
Prepared date: 2026-05-04

## Goal

Update the Business API landing page copy so it no longer targets the EU market, no longer implies licensing/authorization that is not yet available, uses safer vocabulary around funds movement, and aligns the legal/consent wording with the instructed Privacy Notice approach.

## Global rules to apply across the page

1. **Remove EU-targeting and regulation-led marketing.**
   - Do not refer to EU regulation, EU authorization, or the need for a license.
   - Do not say or imply that YouHodler is currently marketing a licensed infrastructure model for EU-regulated crypto-asset services.
   - If a license is obtained in the future, the LP must be reviewed again and must include the required EU disclosures, policies, and legal text before any EU/regulatory claims are added.

2. **Remove or reword `licensed`, `license`, `regulated`, and `compliant` claims.**
   - Remove `licensed` wherever it appears as a descriptor for YouHodler Business API.
   - Remove `regulated infrastructure` wording.
   - Avoid `compliant platform` unless Legal provides exact approved wording.

3. **Use `funds` instead of `money`.**
   - Replace `money movement` with `funds movement`.
   - Replace `move money` with `move funds` or a more precise phrase.
   - Do not use `for others`; it has a negative connotation and should be avoided.

4. **Avoid broad `Agentic Finance` positioning.**
   - `Agentic finance` is too broad.
   - Reword to `automation`, `AI-assisted workflows`, or `automation-ready infrastructure`, depending on context.

5. **Use `internal reporting` only where reporting is mentioned.**
   - Do not imply that YouHodler provides governance or reporting for another legal entity's own company.
   - If reporting is promoted, make it clear that it is internal reporting, activity history, records, or operational visibility.
   - If the intended reporting scope is broader than internal reporting, clarify it with Legal/Product before publishing.

6. **Clarify vague operational terms.**
   - Avoid unclear phrases such as `clear outcomes`.
   - Use more precise wording such as `defined final states`, `clear operation states`, or `settlement visibility`.
   - Avoid vague wording such as `approvals, policies, and audit trail` unless the page explains what approvals, whose policies, and what records are meant.

7. **Verify account segregation before using the claim.**
   - The page currently refers to client accounts as `segregated`.
   - Confirm internally that client accounts are actually marked/treated as segregated before keeping this wording.
   - If not confirmed, replace with a safer phrase such as `account-level tracking` or `separate client account structure`.

8. **Legal/consent wording must use Privacy Notice.**
   - Insert the Privacy Notice. For now, use the existing YouHodler Privacy Notice: https://app.youhodler.com/docs/youhodler_privacy_notice.pdf
   - This can be linked as a PDF or recreated as a web page.
   - If cookies are used on the LP, include the Cookie Policy as well.
   - There are currently no `Terms of Service`; use `Terms` instead.
   - Replace `Privacy Policy` wording with `Privacy Notice` wording.
